How To Baseball

    The basics to learning

  1. MLB

    2. If you are looking to learn, I would recommend watching MLB games. The MLB is Major League Baseball. They have color commentators and all the players are very good. You could pick one player and watch them through the good and the bad. You can see how they bounce back or how they prosper in times of good.

  2. Movies

    3. The movies you can watch to learn baseball are the sandlot, 42, and angles in the outfield. All of these movies feature real baseball situations and are also very entertaining.
